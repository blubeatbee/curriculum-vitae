Votémon

Votémon is a web game, similar to Scrandle, where an user gets to pick which out of two Pokémons they like more.
This fan-made project came about during a Hackathon on January 30th, and since then my team and I have been contributing here and there. Currently not functioning...

This project is not affiliated with Nintendo or GameFreak, and we don't claim any ownership over the copyright.
